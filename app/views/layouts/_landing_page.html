How it works

BumptUp verifies that you are an employee of your company through your work email address. After that, you can give your company the feedback it needs about its direction and its employees. All of your actions on BumptUp are private. No one else even knows you use the system if you don't let them know.

How it helps

Your company needs real, honest, real time feedback from the people who know best: you and your coworkers. While it's easy enough to give praise, it is more difficult to give constructive feedback about what needs to improve. Yet, constructive feedback is what helps your company the most and what your coworkers yearn for. BumptUp tears down the barriers to providing this feedback with a system that allows the feedback to come from anonymized sources within the organization.
